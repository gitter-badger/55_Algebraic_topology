\subsection{Paracompact space examples}
Locally compact Hausdorff spaces that are also 
\PMlinkname{$\sigma$-compact}{AlternativeDefinitionOfSigmaFiniteMeasure} can be shown to be {\em paracompact},
and, therefore, also normal. Other particular cases of paracompact spaces are those of locally compact Hausdorff spaces, second countable Hausdorff locally compact spaces, and their related locally compact groupoids.
