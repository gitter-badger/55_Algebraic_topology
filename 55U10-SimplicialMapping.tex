Let $K$ and $L$ be simplicial complexes and $f: K \to L$.
If for each simplex $\sigma^n$ of $K$, we have that $f(\sigma^n)$ is a simplex
of $L$, then $f$ is said to be a \emph{simplicial mapping}.
