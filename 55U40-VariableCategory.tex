\begin{definition}  

\emph{A variable category} is defined as an indexed family or class of either categories and, or functor categories $[\G_i]$ with $i \in I$ in the functor category $\F_{ct}$ of categories and functors with additional axioms, rules, or properties of the underlying categories, that specify for example how such categories can be 'freely generated'(\cite{BBG2k7a}).
\end{definition}
