round complexity


Mimicking the Lusternik-Schnirelmann category invariant for a smooth manifold M we can ask about the minimal number of critical loops of smooth scalar maps M→ℝ which are round functions, that is functions whose critical points are aligned in a disjoint union of closed curves (a link).

This number is called the round complexity of M and it is symbolized as roc⁢(M)

Then

roc(M)=min#{critical loops of f | f:M→ℝ is round function}

This concept is related to the invariant called t-cat.
